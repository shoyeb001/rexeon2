ABOUT US

Rexeon Solutions Private Limited provides NABL accredited testing services and certifications like NABL,NABCB and others . It has been established amidst the outbreak of Covid-19 on 23rd of June. It is headquartered in Dhanbad, Jharkhand. We are a team of technically competent manpower having combined industry work experiences of over 8 years in diverse fields.
Rexeon Solutions has a support of vast number of labs specializing in different fields. These fields include food,water, mineral, soil, environmental and many more. Our collaboration with these labs allows our clients to avail testing service at the same price, thereby providing them options according to their requirements.
With the help of these labs, the products are analyzed as per the global standards which allow our client’s business to be competitive. Other than testing, we also help them to get all the necessary certifications (such as ISO, FSSAI, etc.) for the growth of their business.
Rexeon provides state-of-art analysis with high precision and accuracy using modern technology. Our associated labs are accredited by ISO and National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration. Our most knowledgeable and experienced hands will offer customized solutions.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to ease the access of testing for even small scale and micro enterprises. We also aim to provide them the required certifications needed to grow their business and help them in their decision making.

OUR VISION
